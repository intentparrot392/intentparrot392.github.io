About me

Yo! I’m Bird, a 13-year-old developer and creator who’s been coding and making stuff online for a few years now.

I started when I was about 9, just playing around with C# and Lua while trying to understand how games worked. A year later, I got into Roblox Studio and began making small games and scripts. That’s when I really started to get hooked.

Around 11, I picked up Java and started experimenting with plugins and random tools. By 12, I had learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which helped me build websites like this one. Been coding for about 4 years now and still going strong.

Besides coding, I also make Roblox videos and post them on YouTube and TikTok. I love mixing my programming skills with content creation. It’s all about having fun, learning new things, and sharing what I build.

Right now, I’m working on a few game ideas and expanding my projects while growing my online stuff. Just trying to create cool things that people enjoy and level up a bit more every day.
